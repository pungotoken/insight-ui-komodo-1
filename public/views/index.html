What is STBL?

STBL is a fee-less stable token that is backed by other stable coins. STBL acts as a proxy token and can be swapped for other coins directly within Pungo Cash

Pungo Cash STBL is aiming to empower our users to interact with our products while facilitating the usage of stable coins for main stream users.

This explorer is currently showing the live STBL network!
